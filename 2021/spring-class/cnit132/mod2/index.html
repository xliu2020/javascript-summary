Recommendations for SEO

  • Take advantage of online SEO tools to see if you have good titles. In order to increasing both the quality and quantity of website traffic, it is a good way to analyse your website. For example, using online SEO tool to give you an objective professional analysis of your website and some data of how your titles work well then give you an idea on how to make it better.
  • Take advantage of web crawler. If you are using websites for business or personal branding, web crawler can manage your website for you to reduce your workload and help you to monitor the news and social media sites of yours and your competitors as well as collecting competitive information for you.
  • Google doesn't use keywords in meta tags. Google, which has the biggest users group in the world does not use the keywords meta tags in web rankings because too many people use it for spam. Therefore, don't worry about other people to use your name in their meta tags. But it doesn't mean Google doesn't use meta tags at all. Google will take meta description as reference and use part of the information as snippet or will use meta tags in Google applications.
  • Social Media & SEO. To promote your brands and bring more traffic to your websites, you should post your website link as well as keywords of your website on social media website to "guide" customers to visit your website and make full use of meta tags to direct the social media page to show exactly what you want to be shown.
